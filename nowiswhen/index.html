Now Is When?
(Tanah Air Suci Kami)
تانه اير سوچي كامي

Syahrul Anuar
2020
2 Channel Video Installation
Dimensions Variable

Utilising the artist’s cultural and historical roots, Now Is When? reflects upon the changing perception and practicality of granite rocks, within the Nusantara region. Through the intersection of geologic sciences, cultural fables, Pantuns and contemporary footages, the artist melds together an ethnographic investigation of his own roots into a two-channel video installation.

Shifting away from the rhetorics of absolute poetry and aestheticism — Now Is When? juggles upon both fact / fiction and science / myth to delve further into a cultural psyche. Through granite as a means of a conceptual pivot, the artist dives further into its mysticism and its historical intertwining within the Nusantara Folklore.

Drawing upon a personal fascination of cultural spiritualism, religious fanaticism and ethnic nomadism within the Nusantara region, Now Is When? becomes a visual investigation of the pre-colonial amnestic past and perennial stereotypes — of a history altered and forgotten.
