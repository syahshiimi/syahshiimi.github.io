Everything is Temporary

4x5" Album Photographs
Artist Book
2019

Memories are fragile; subjected to change by the beholder either consciously or subconsciously. It is fraught with unreliability and yet, memories are what we ever hold so tight to. The nascent feeling of trawling old images, photographs exist as a manifestation of a desire — to grapple onto our own past and what it means to have been existing.

EVERYTHING IS TEMPORARY plays upon the inability to let go. Documenting the trip religiously, the photobook exists as the beacon as to what it really means to be afraid of losing something. Precious memories that are highly personal and yet of no value to another person but the self.

The feeling of sentimentality and nostalgia pervades throughout this body of work. EVERYTHING IS TEMPORARY investigates the artist’s own inability to let go and instead using the desire to re-possess as a means to cope in a world that is changing.
