Background Noise

Photographic Scores
Max MSP
2019

It is estimated that only 4% of Singapore is in total darkness. Light pollution exists as a paradox in Singapore, where it allows us to see at night, yet blinding us from seeing the stars.

Background Noise is a series of photographic images, that utilises the digital camera as a means to interrogate light pollution in Singapore. The digital noise in the image exists as a byproduct of the sensor as it increases its photographic sensitivity became a point of departure in this series.

Through creative coding, Background Noise attempts to envision an alternative means of encountering an image. How can we instead hear what we cannot see?
