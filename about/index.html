• About Work

Often contemplating upon narratives and histories, Syahrul Anuar (b. 1995 Singapore) surveys and unravels the complexities of how humans consume, depict and attempt understand it. Pondering upon the fact and fictions that permeates through narratives and history — his practice employs extensive and fluid use of mediums as his visual language.

He is currently pursuing a Bachelor’s of Fine Arts at the Nanyang Technological University, School of Art, Design Media. He has shown his works in galleries across Singapore such as DECK and Objectifs and is currently pursuing to expand his artistic practice and presence on an international level.
