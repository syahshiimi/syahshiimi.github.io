Biography

Contemplating upon narratives and histories, Syahrul Anuar (b. 1995 Singapore) surveys, unravels and weaves the complexities and multiplicities of how humans consume, depict and attempt convey it. Pondering upon the fact and fictions that permeates through narratives and history — his practice employs an extensive and fluid use of mediums as his visual language.

He is a recent graduate at the Nanyang Technological University, School of Art, Design Media with a Bachelor’s of Fine Arts. He has shown his works in galleries across Singapore such as DECK and Objectifs and is currently pursuing to expand his artistic practice and presence to an international audience.
